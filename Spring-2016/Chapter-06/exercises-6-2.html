Exercise 1

fprintf('Determine if ')
v1 = [2, 1, 0]
v2 = [-1, 1, 3]
fprintf('and')
v3 = [0, -1, 6]
fprintf('spans the vector space of rows with three real entries which has\n')
fprintf('dimension 3. Record your results below.')
A = [2, -1, 0; 1, 1, -1; 0, 3, 6]
rref_of_A = rref(A)
